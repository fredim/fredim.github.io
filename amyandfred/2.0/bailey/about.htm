Bailey came into our lives on October 25, 2005. He's a purebred lemon beagle who absolutely loves people. We purchased him from a wonderful breader in Missouri. He had his first plane ride and did great.

On the day of this posting, he is 11-weeks old and as curious as ever. He wants to meet everyone and wants to put everything he can into his mouth.

We're in the process of crate training him right now, and I don't think he likes it much. But we're going to nail this potty training thing in the butt. If anyone has any suggestions, we're open to hearing them. We think he's starting to get the hang of it, but then he surprises us with a little tinkle by the door. *sigh* Patience is a virtue.
