Thomas Fogarty Winery was the first place we visited the day after Fred proposed by the beautiful ocean at Moss Beach Distrillery in Half Moon Bay. We instantly fell in love with the winery, nestled atop the Santa Cruz Moutains and overlooking the entire Bay Area. We knew that we wanted to have an outdoor wedding and decided that a winery provided the romantic atmosphere that could transend us into the wedding of our dreams. We looked at several other wineries, but none compared with the breathtaking view that Fogarty had to offer...and so it was decided. We are so excited to have our guests experience it!
