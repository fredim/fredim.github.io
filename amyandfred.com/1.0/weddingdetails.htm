OFFICIANT

Father Gerald Caprio

Fr. Gerry Caprio came to the San Francisco Bay Area from his native New Jersey eight years ago. He was ordained a priest in 1970 and has served in many ministries over the years. These include inner city parish work in Newark, NJ, teaching in high school and college - including Seton Hall University, hospital chaplaincy and suburban parish assignments. He loves California and when not officiating at Weddings and Baptisms he enjoys hiking through the trails around Mount Tamalpais.
 
ENTERTAINMENT

Black Market Jazz Orchestra
The Black Market Jazz Orchestra is a sophisticated dance band that plays music from every era. The band was originally formed in 1993 to record a CD entitled "Art Attack" for Sea Breeze records. With the success of the CD and the popularity of the hand rising they were soon called to perform with name entertainers such as Marilyn McCoo, Ray Charles, Connie Stevens, and Tony Bennett.
