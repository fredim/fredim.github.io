For those of you traveling from out of town and need a place to stay, we have made arrangements for group rates at the Westin Palo Alto. The Westin is an ideal location as it is situated in walking distance from downtown Palo Alto. It is also a suggested pick up/drop off point for any people who are considering carpooling up to Fogarty. There are different driving routes to take to get to Thomas Fogarty Winery: the direct route is about a 14 miles drive and will take on average 30 minutes from the Westin.
