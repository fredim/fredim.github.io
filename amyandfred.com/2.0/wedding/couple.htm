Fred and Amy first met back in college at UC Berkeley. Amy was good friends with several of the guys in Fred's fraternity. At first, however, Amy and Fred did not hit it off. Amy didn't like Fred and always kept her distance. She thought he was an extremely rude person and very arrogant. They avoided each other for a few years.

Towards the end of Amy's Junior year at Cal however, their relationship took an odd turn. They started to talk more at group gatherings. With the advent of Yahoo IM, they talked more and more over instant message and would go out to happy hours after work. Friendship, however was all that it was. Amy and Fred soon had to battle rumors about their relationship. But soon, those rumors actually became truth as the two began to get to know each other more, and started to fall in love.

Their courtship came as a shock to a lot of people who remember how Amy and Fred first met years ago. But surprise, here they are today....married and in love.
